My name is Ruolin Ye. I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Cornell University supervised by Prof. Tapomayukh Bhattacharjee. If you find any research interest we may share, or some intersection on projects I am working on, please feel free to drop me an Email. I am always open to collaboration.
